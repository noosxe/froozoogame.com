Mission:

We are a group of ordinary youngsters fancy coders who gathered together to create a great “Match 3” game. When we started designing the game we were happy to realise that we all share one same passion. That was not the iPhone 6, or a brand new Ferrari, but a love for animals and the will to protect them. We decided to share our vision and encourage many others to help protect and rescue animals all around the world the way no one did before - with Froo Zoo game. We’ve done it. We are young and we can make difference.

We want to support initiatives that will lead to belief and understanding among children and youngsters of the ultimate need to achieve long-term protection of wildlife and the environment, and in stopping the abuse and exploitation of animals held in captivity.
